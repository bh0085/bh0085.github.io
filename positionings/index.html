Positioning Stories

Five strategic narratives for SSI-Newco

Positioning 1

The Ladder to the Moon

"Operational superintelligence through embodied AI is the necessary foundation for achieving scientific superintelligence—we're building that foundation."

  • Current AI approaches are building ladders to the moon.
  • We combine sensory systems (XR) + reasoning (AI) + physical action (robotics).
  • Two product tracks prove the platform (LabOS & ClinOS).
Positioning 2

The Data Differentiation

"We're unlocking scientific breakthroughs by generating the operational data that AI needs but can't currently access."

  • The AI revolution has a data bottleneck.
  • Our XR + robotics platform generates novel training data.
  • First products (LabOS/ClinOS) create data moats.
Positioning 3

The Scientific Infrastructure

"We're building the operating system for automated science—starting with the lab, expanding to the clinic."

  • Labs and clinical trials are stuck in manual operations.
  • LabOS is the first OS for laboratory automation.
  • Platform architecture enables rapid expansion.
Positioning 4

The Company vs. Product

"Investors often fund products; we're offering them a company that builds multiple products on a transformative platform."

  • The "tracks" framework signals platform thinking from day one.
  • Each track validates the core technology while serving distinct markets.
  • Network effects across tracks compound value.
